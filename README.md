# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
Project Objective

The HR Dashboard provides insights into workforce composition, employee satisfaction, salary distribution, and termination trends. The dashboard enables HR teams to monitor employee metrics and make informed workforce decisions.

Business Questions Answered

The dashboard answers the following questions:

- What is the total number of employees since inception?
- How many employees are currently active?
- How many employees have been terminated?
- What is the employee termination rate?
- What is the total salary paid?
- What is the gender distribution of current employees?
- What are the education levels of employees?
- How satisfied are employees with their jobs?
- What is the employee work-life balance distribution?
- How many employees were hired each quarter?
- How many employees were terminated each quarter?
- What are the monthly termination trends?
- How does average salary change over time?
- How does salary vary based on years at the company and employment type?
- How do total working years and salary differ by gender?
